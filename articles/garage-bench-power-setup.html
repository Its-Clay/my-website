How I Built My Garage Bench Power Setup

Published Feb 1, 2026

I wanted a compact bench setup that could handle chargers, test gear, and one surprise load without tripping breakers. The key upgrades were a switched power strip, clear cable routing, and labeling everything up front.

If I had to summarize the whole build: prioritize safety first, convenience second, and expandability third. It is way easier to start simple and add capability than to unwind a complicated layout later.
