Welcome to itsclay.net

A calm corner of the internet where I document projects, experiments, and the occasional “how did I end up here?” rabbit hole.

Feb 19, 2026 ~3 minute read Notes · Projects · Systems

I built this site to be simple, fast, and quietly ambitious: a place for write-ups that are useful now, and still readable a year from now.

What this site is: practical notes, polished lessons, and a growing archive of things I’ve built.
What it isn’t: a feed. No doomscroll. Just good pages.

What you’ll find here

Articles

Short, high-signal write-ups: how something works, what I learned, and what I’d do differently.

Projects

Build logs for A/V, IT, maker stuff, and whatever new tool I’m obsessed with this month.

Experiments

Little tests, comparisons, and prototypes that don’t deserve a full “project,” but are still worth saving.

Notes

A living notebook: the small details that future-me will thank present-me for writing down.

Where it’s going

The north star is a library: pages that load instantly, look great, and age well. Over time I’ll add richer media — diagrams, photos, and maybe a few tasteful animations — but I’m keeping the content first.

A note on craft

I’m aiming for “small and intentional.” If I add animation, it’ll serve the story — and it’ll respect your system settings (so if you prefer reduced motion, the site will too).

If you’re reading this: thanks. This is the first brick in the wall.
