3 Lessons From Rebuilding a Used Dirt Bike

Published Jan 17, 2026

Lesson one: spend more time inspecting than wrenching in the first week. The notes you take up front save days later. Lesson two: buy common failure parts before teardown. Lesson three: track each milestone in photos.

Rebuilds can feel endless, so I now split projects into short checkpoints that still move the bike toward first start. Momentum matters just as much as torque specs.
